Discover Your Consciousness Architecture

Your conscious experience emerges from a sophisticated prediction machine that constantly balances precision-weighted sensory signals against metabolic costs. This assessment maps your consciousness architecture across three core APGI parameters:

  • Ignition Threshold: How much precision-weighted prediction error is needed to trigger conscious awareness
  • Interoceptive Precision: How much you trust (weight) signals from your body vs. external world
  • Somatic Integration: How strongly bodily predictions shape your conscious experience

These aren't personality traits—they're computational parameters that can shift based on context, training, and metabolic state. Answer based on your typical resting state when calm and alert.
