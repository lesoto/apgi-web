About This Book

"When Consciousness Turns On" explores the fascinating journey of how consciousness emerges and develops in the human mind. With over 300 color illustrations, this groundbreaking work takes readers through the intricate processes that awaken our awareness and shape our perception of reality.

A comprehensive guide to understanding the nature of consciousness and the mind's remarkable capacity for self-awareness.
