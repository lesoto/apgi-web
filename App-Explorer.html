Four Core Parameters

Understanding the fundamental dimensions of your cognitive processing

ε

Prediction Error Sensitivity

Measures how sensitive you are to unexpected events, novelty, and changes in your environment. High sensitivity drives exploration and learning.

π

Precision Allocation

Analyzes how you prioritize different information sources—sensory input, bodily signals, or internal models—when making decisions.

θₜ

Ignition Threshold

Assesses the level of evidence needed for information to reach conscious awareness and trigger focused attention.

β

Somatic Bias

Evaluates your attunement to bodily sensations, interoceptive signals, and how physical states influence your decisions.
