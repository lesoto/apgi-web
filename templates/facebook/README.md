# APGI Facebook Templates

## Overview
This package contains professionally designed templates for Facebook marketing and content creation, following the APGI Framework's visual identity and communication principles.

## Contents

### 1. Post Templates
- **Standard Post**: 1200x630px optimized for news feed
- **Square Post**: 1080x1080px for mobile-first engagement
- **Landscape Post**: 1640x924px for desktop viewing

### 2. Story Templates
- **Story Template**: 1080x1920px full-screen vertical format
- **Poll Story**: Interactive template with poll integration
- **Quiz Story**: Engagement-focused quiz templates

### 3. Cover Templates
- **Page Cover**: 851x315px for Facebook pages
- **Group Cover**: 1640x856px for Facebook groups
- **Event Cover**: 1920x1080px for Facebook events

### 4. Ad Templates
- **Single Image Ad**: Various sizes for different placements
- **Carousel Ad**: Multi-product showcase templates
- **Video Ad Thumbnail**: Optimized for video campaigns

## Design Guidelines

### Colors
- Primary: #1e40af (APGI Blue)
- Secondary: #0d9488 (APGI Teal)
- Accent: #7c3aed (APGI Purple)
- Background: #ffffff (White)
- Text: #0f172a (Dark Blue)

### Typography
- Headings: Space Grotesk (700 weight)
- Body: Inter (400-500 weights)
- Accent: Playfair Display (for quotes)

### Voice & Tone
- **Provocative**: Challenge conventional thinking
- **Authoritative**: Evidence-based confidence
- **Compassionate**: Human-centered approach

## Usage Instructions

### For Designers
1. Open the appropriate template file in your design software
2. Replace placeholder text with your content
3. Maintain brand colors and typography
4. Export in PNG or JPG format

### For Marketers
1. Choose the template that matches your content type
2. Customize while maintaining brand consistency
3. Test different variations for optimal engagement
4. Follow Facebook's advertising guidelines

## Best Practices

### Content Strategy
- Use provocative questions to spark engagement
- Include authoritative statistics and research
- Show compassionate human stories
- Maintain consistent visual identity

### Technical Specifications
- File size: Under 100KB for optimal loading
- Format: PNG for transparency, JPG for photos
- Resolution: 72 DPI for web, 300 DPI for print
- Color profile: sRGB for digital displays

### Engagement Tips
- Post during peak hours (2-4 PM weekdays)
- Use relevant hashtags (#APGI #Consciousness #Neuroscience)
- Include clear call-to-actions
- Respond to comments within 2 hours
