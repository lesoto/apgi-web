## Technical Specifications

### Video Requirements
- **Format**: MP4, MOV, or WebM
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920px (recommended)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16
- **Frame Rate**: 23-60 FPS
- **Duration**: 15-60 seconds optimal
- **File Size**: Under 287MB

### Audio Requirements
- **Format**: AAC, MP3, or WAV
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1kHz
- **Bitrate**: 128kbps or higher
- **Volume**: Normalized to -6dB

### Text Requirements
- **Font Size**: Minimum 44px for readability
- **Contrast**: High contrast with background
- **Position**: Keep text within safe zones
- **Duration**: Text on screen for 2-3 seconds minimum
