# APGI Instagram Templates

## Overview
This package contains visually stunning templates for Instagram content creation, designed to maximize engagement while maintaining the APGI Framework's brand identity and communication principles.

### 1. Post Templates
- **Square Post**: 1080x1080px - Instagram's classic format
- **Portrait Post**: 1080x1350px - Vertical carousel content
- **Carousel Post**: Multi-slide templates for storytelling

### 2. Story Templates
- **Standard Story**: 1080x1920px - Full-screen vertical format
- **Interactive Story**: Poll, quiz, and question templates
- **Series Story**: Multi-part story templates for campaigns

### 3. Reel Templates
- **Reel Cover**: 1080x1920px - Eye-catching thumbnail templates
- **Reel Overlay**: Text and graphic overlays for video content

- **IGTV Cover**: 1080x1920px - Long-form video thumbnails
- **IGTV Series**: Branded series templates

## Design Guidelines

- **Gradient**: Primary to accent gradient for visual impact

- **Imagery**: Scientific yet approachable visuals

### Content Strategy
- **Provocative**: Challenge conventional wisdom about consciousness

### Instagram-Specific Best Practices

## Usage Instructions

### For Content Creators
1. Select template based on content type
2. Customize colors and text while maintaining brand consistency
3. Add high-quality imagery or illustrations
4. Include relevant hashtags and mentions
5. Schedule posts for optimal engagement times

### For Designers
1. Maintain 1080px width for all templates
2. Use safe zones for text and important elements
3. Export in PNG format for quality
4. Test on mobile devices before publishing
5. Follow Instagram's content guidelines

## Technical Specifications

### Image Requirements
- **Format**: PNG for quality, JPG for file size
- **Resolution**: 72 DPI for web
- **Color Profile**: sRGB
- **File Size**: Under 30MB for uploads

### Video Requirements
- **Format**: MP4 or MOV
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920px (vertical)
- **Frame Rate**: 30 FPS
- **Duration**: 15-90 seconds for Reels
