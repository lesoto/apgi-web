What This Shows

Your APGI Signature describes how your brain regulates awareness through four computational parameters:

  • Prediction Error Sensitivity (ε): How intensely you register mismatches between expectation and reality
  • Precision Allocation (π): Which signals (threat, reward, body, social) your brain trusts most
  • Ignition Threshold (θₜ): How much signal is needed to reach conscious awareness (lower = easier access)
  • Somatic Bias (β): Whether experience is anchored in bodily sensation or external cognition

This is not a diagnosis. Your signature shifts with context, stress, sleep, and training.
