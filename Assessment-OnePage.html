Research & Clinical Context:

This assessment measures parameters within the APGI (Allostatic Precision-Gated Ignition) theoretical framework. Results should be interpreted within appropriate research or clinical contexts. This is not a diagnostic tool.

What This Measures:

Prediction Error (ε): Mismatch between expected and actual sensory/interoceptive input
Precision Weighting (π): Confidence/reliability assigned to prediction errors
Ignition Threshold (θ): Critical threshold for conscious access via global broadcasting
Somatic Bias (β): Relative weighting of interoceptive vs. exteroceptive signals
