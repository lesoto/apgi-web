What This Assessment Measures

The APGI (Activation-Precision-Gating-Integration) framework models consciousness as four interacting parameters that shape your experience:

ε - Prediction Error Sensitivity
How sensitive you are to surprises and mismatches
π - Precision Allocation
How much you trust different signals
θₜ - Ignition Threshold
How much signal is needed for conscious awareness
β - Somatic Bias
Whether awareness anchors in body or world

This assessment measures:

  • All four APGI parameters
  • Nonlinear interactions between parameters
  • Temporal stability (transient vs. enduring vs. entrenched)
  • Environmental fit and context sensitivity

Environmental Fit Principle

No signature is inherently "better" - optimal configuration depends on context. A high threshold is great for focused work but bad for detecting subtle threats.

⚠️ Important: This is a tool based on theoretical neuroscience, not a clinical diagnostic.
